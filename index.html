Gymnastics Club

Mistral Gymnastics Club was formed in September 2006 and is registered with British Gymnastics, the sportâ€™s governing body. We are a non-profit-making organisation and run courses in general gymnastics and trampolining at the Taro Leisure Centre in Petersfield, Hampshire.

At Mistral Gymnastics Club, our philosophy is: Make it safe, make it fun, make it happen.

Make it safe
Safety for our gymnasts is paramount. We want you to feel comfortable that your child is in safe hands whilst they are with us and therefore, we take all possible precautions to make sure that they come to no harm. At the moment, we have a ratio of 3 coaches/helpers to 18 children and we use recognised progressions to teach various skills to reduce the risk of accident / injury.
Make it fun
It is important to us that our gymnasts enjoy themselves and that they want to keep coming. We provide a varied programme throughout the term to enable our gymnasts to learn new skills whilst having some fun.
Make it happen
We recognise that every child is an individual with different skills and talents. We work with each child to help them to achieve their personal goals whether it is learning to catch a ball or performing a sequence of moves on the trampoline. Throughout the term, the gymnasts will be working towards the British Gymnastics Awards in the various gymnastic disciplines so that they have clearly defined goals. In the future, it is hoped that there will be opportunities for our gymnasts to take part in competitions against other clubs.

Classes

We run recreational classes in two gymnastics disciplines for children aged from 4-11 years.

General Gymnastics
Our general gymnastics programme helps children to acquire various skills using various apparatus such as a vaulting box, bars, beam, floor and trampoline. Using recognised progressions for the safe teaching of gymnastics, children work towards the British Gymnastics Award Schemes.
Saturday 08:30 – 09:30
Saturday 09:30 – 10:30
Trampolining
Our Trampolining programme is designed for those gymnasts who are interested in developing the trampoline skills that they have learned in the recreational gymnastic classes further. In addition to working for the British Gymnastics Trampoline Awards, the children also learn how to perform routines.
Saturday 10:30 – 11:30
Mistral Minis
Mistral Minis is our new class specifically for children aged 4 to 5 years, who are in either reception or year 1 at primary school. Mistral Minis follows our structured ‘Rainbow’ programme which provides a foundation towards achieving the British Gymnastics Proficency Awards and Trampoline Awards.
Friday 16:45 – 18:45
